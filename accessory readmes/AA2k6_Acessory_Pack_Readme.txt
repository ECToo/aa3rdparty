Accessory Pack for AA2k6
--------------------------------------
These are all the maps, models, and skins released by people in the community.  I made this so it'd be easier to
get everything all at once instead of scouring the forums, forsakenweb, and CORPS.  This readme gives credit to the
authors of everything in this pack.
(I'm not sure who to give credit to on the music, I'm just giving credit to whomever released it with their map.)
